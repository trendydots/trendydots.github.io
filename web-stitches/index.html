A web application that helps you rapidly design a landing page or an editorial website. It takes seconds to experiment with different layouts.







Helping Europe's creative minds



During 24 - 27 April 2020, web-stitches was used by many teams who participated to the European Commission's #EUvsVirus hackathon. The teams were able to rapidly build presentation pages as well as mobile showcases that demonstrate their awesome ideas on how to create new services and products that help people around the world during the COVID-19 pandemic.
