The right shoe for the right occasion

If you agree to participate in my experiment, I kindly ask you to take the role of a teacher, helping a young student understand the basic concept of wearing the right shoe for the right occasion.

Method

As it's teacher, plase help our Gaston make correct associations between season, material and types of shoes.
You will be presented with a "Dos" and a "Donts" section. Each section contans multiple cathegories with notions that need to be associated. Add 10 Dos and 10 Donts.
