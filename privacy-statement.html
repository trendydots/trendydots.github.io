1. Personal data that we process

Trendy Dots processes your personal data because you use our services and / or because you have provided it to us yourself. Information that Trendy Dots may need from you (or is necessary for the purposes described farther below) and which could be processed by Trendy Dots, concerns:
   • Initials and / or first name and last name
   • Company name
   • Address details (consisting of street name + house number + ZIP code + country)
   • Phone number
   • Bank details (only when you submit them in Trendy Dot's payment forms)
   • E-mail address
   • IP-address (as recorded by Google Analytics)
   • Data about your activity on this website
   • Internet browser and device type
The data you input when using the Trendy Dots platforms is considered private, but not personal.

2. Does Trendy Dots also process special personal data?

Special personal data are sensitive data, such as data about health or a criminal records, ethnic data or data concerning race. Trendy Dots does not process special personal data.

3. Why do we need the data?

Once you have an agreement with Trendy Dots, we want to be of service to you. We use your data for the following purposes:
   • Being able to call or email you if necessary to carry out our services.
   • Being able to inform you about changes to our services and products.
   • Being able to to deliver goods and services to you.
   • If we are legally obliged to do this, such as information we need for our tax return.
   • Data can also be used for promotional or marketing purposes, which are proportional to the type of service provided by Trendy Dots. For example to inform you via our newsletter about a new activities of Trendy Dots. If you do not wish to be contacted via our newsletter, you can indicate this in the newsletter by clicking ‘unsubscribe’ at the bottom of each newsletter email.

We can only use your data for a purpose other than that for which they were originally processed, if there is a close relationship between the two goals.

4. Which rules apply to the processing of personal data?

When processing personal data, Trendy Dots is bound by the applicable laws and regulations and keeps track of developments. In that context, Trendy Dots reserves the right to make changes to this privacy statement in order to have the text match the latest state of affairs, as stated at the bottom of this privacy statement.

5. How long we store data?

Trendy Dots will not store your personal data longer than is strictly necessary to achieve the purposes for which your data is collected.

6. Sharing with others / provision of data to third parties

Trendy Dots does not sell your information to third parties and only provides it if this is necessary for the execution of our agreement with you or to comply with a legal obligation. With companies that process your data in our order, we conclude a processor agreement to ensure the same level of security and confidentiality of your data. Trendy Dots shares (only the required personal) data with the following third parties:
   • Google Analytics: to see overall user behavior on this website
   • Gmail: to reply to messages send to suport.trendydots[at]gmail.com and research.trendydots[at]gmail.com
   • Basin: to collect subscriptions from Trendy Dot’s websites
   • Mailchimp: to send newsletters to the email addresses that have actively signed up for Trendy Dots’s newsletter
   • Patreon: to receive your donations which help us build and expand our software

7. Tracking website visits

Trendy Dots only uses technical and functional cookies. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer, tablet or smartphone when you first visit our websites. The cookies we use are necessary for the technical operation of the websites and your ease of use. They ensure that the websites work properly. You can opt out of cookies by setting your internet browser so that it does not store cookies anymore. In addition, you can also delete all information previously saved via the settings of your browser.

8. View, modify or delete data

Of course you always have the opportunity to view, correct or delete all your personal data known to us. For this you send a specified request to support.trendydots[at]gmail.com

Trendy Dots has taken appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data and protect it against loss, alteration, misuse or against any (other) form of unlawful processing. Any person who has access to personal data and / or processes personal data at Trendy Dots is obliged to maintain the confidentiality of that data, unless otherwise provided by law or regulation. In case Trendy Dots makes use of the services of third parties, Trendy Dots will make agreements regarding the necessary security measures in the context of the protection of personal data. If you have the impression that your data is not properly secured or if there are indications of abuse, please contact us at support.trendydots[at]gmail.com

10. Your privacy rights

When you submit a request for inspection, correction, objection or deletion of your personal data, Trendy Dots will inform you about this within 25 working days. You can submit this request via suport.trendydots[at]gmail.com.
